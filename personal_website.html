Justin J. Van Ee

Intro

Hello! I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Agricultural Biology at Colorado State University advised by Cynthia Brown and Mevin Hooten. My current research focuses on building joint models for genomics, experimental, and phenomenological data to understand and predict the invasion of cheatgrass in western ecosystems of the United States. More broadly, I am interested in developing computationally efficient hierarchical models for assimilating eclectic data sources.
